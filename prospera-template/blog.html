Why Organic?

Healthy food is the right of every living organism on the earth. That's why micro organisms attack first on it and farmer apply chemical toxins or poisons to kill them. These poisens are called chemical pesticides.

India is in top 10 countries who consume agro pesticides most. Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh consumes around 50 percent pesticides of total consumption in India. They feed vegetables and fruits to whole North India.
Organic farming technique gives solution to save the food without using toxins. .

 

 

 

What is Organic Farming?

Organic Farming is not new for India. In ancient time, farming was done in India organically. Organic farminng is a natural way of farming.

There are 2 basic steps of farming - feeding of nutrients and pest control.

In this techniq, nutrients are provided to the plant in natural form rather than derived suppliments. They are feeded to the plants continuously and natural way.

 

 

 

Are Terrace and Balcony good place for Farming?

Why Waste Management?

Urban dump yard is collecting around 14000 tonnes of solid mix waste daily. Delhi MCD is looking for the solution since dump yard is getting filled. According to an assessment by the Centre for Science and Environment (CSE), every tonne of waste disposed of at a landfill would cost the MCD about Rs 14,500. Mean, the cost to dispose from house to landfill of Delhi is about 20 crores daily. Our small effort can easily resolve this issue.
