About Us

Mission

We want every households to utilise their free space into productive space like home garden and enhance their contribution in environmental protection as well. Our aim not only ensure but assure everyone that the food they are growing and consuming is safe, healthy and organic right beneath the roof in front of their own eyes.

In the current scenario of ground water level depletion, leading to water stress condition our mission also tackles the optimal use of water and help in saving precious water resource.

 

 

 

 

 

Our Story

People in India are facing a serious issue of lack of quality vegetables in the market. Vegetables available in the market are usually, highly contaminated with hazardous pesticides.

Organic food is very difficult to find in the market and is not affordable to all due to its significantly high cost. Organic certification of food products does not provide reliable assurance of safety due to corruption in the certification system.

In 2019, we started Let's Farm with a dream to fulfill this gap and resolve the problems faced by the consumers. We at Let's Farm, provide complete installation of kitchen garden by transforming the free space in the house into mini farms. All the mini farms are designed by us as per the requirements and needs of the family. Production of vegetable from these mini farms fulfill the need of vegetables for the whole family.

Not only do we install them, but also take care of the mini farms. Complete maintenance and management solutions provided by us follow complete organic farming practices, so that the mini farms produce healthy vegetables which are free from any kind of contamination.
